Good Sight Will

Objective

Hello, my name is William 'Good Sight Will' King. I'm a husband father and aspiring Singer/Song Writer and Recording Artist, made and raised by the city of South Minneapolis, in and around the landmark so famously known to the world as, the George Floyd square.

As an educator and motivator at heart, I turned to creating music back in early 2019, both, as an outlet and alternative to a career in the line of ministry. With understanding culture as a foundation to education, I decided to combine the hip hop, R&B, culture, education and my new found talent, with biblical truths. Creating a sound of vibe that picks up where some of the greatest men and women of the past century left off. Uplifting and Empowering those to come up after us.

  • Music is the art of my breath, spewing life and giving hope.
